Snake Game
## Overview ##
The Snake Game AI project is a simple but interesting artificial intelligence 
project where we train a computer program to play the classic Snake game automatically.
In this project, the AI learns how to control the snake, eat food, and avoid crashing into walls or its own body. 

## How It Works ##
The main idea is to use *Reinforcement Learning*, where the AI improves through trial and error.
It receives a positive reward when it eats food and a negative reward when it dies or makes a wrong move.
Over time, the AI learns the best actions to survive longer and score higher.

## Learning Process ##

At first, the AI plays randomly without any strategy. However, after many training sessions,
it gradually becomes smarter and starts playing the game more efficiently by learning from its experiences.


## Technologies Used ##
- Python 🐍  
- Pygame 🎮 (for game development)  
- NumPy 🔢

## Goal of the Project ##

- Train an AI to play Snake automatically  
- Improve decision-making using rewards and penalties  
- Increase survival time and score through learning

## Outcome ##
After training, the AI becomes capable of playing the Snake game efficiently, 
making smart decisions, and achieving higher scores compared to random gameplay.
